Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

The Scattered Nest ("we", "us", or "our") operates an online store selling print-on-demand merchandise and digital products. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in connection with our Pinterest integration.

Information We Collect

Our Pinterest integration accesses only our own Pinterest business account to publish pins and read board information. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from Pinterest users or visitors.

How We Use Information

We use the Pinterest API solely to automate the scheduling and publishing of pins that promote our own products on our own Pinterest boards. No user data is collected, shared, or sold.

Third-Party Services

We sell products through Redbubble and Payhip. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing transactions made on their sites. We do not receive or store payment information from customers.

Data Storage

We do not maintain any databases of user information. Our Pinterest API credentials are stored securely and are used only for automated pin publishing on our own account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our Pinterest profile: @TheScatteredNest.
