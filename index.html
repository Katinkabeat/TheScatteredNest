Our Story

Life's a little messy. We love it that way.

The Scattered Nest started with two dogs and a whole lot of sticky notes. Zero is a one-eyed double merle Border Collie who navigates the world without sight or sound, and somehow still runs the household. Scotty is a Miniature Pinscher-Chihuahua mix with the confidence of a Great Dane and zero concept of personal space. They're both sensitive to the big wide world and have a lot of feelings about other dogs, but at home? Absolute angels. Best friends. Total couch hogs.

Keeping track of their vet visits, medications, behaviours, and training progress started as survival, and turned into something worth sharing. I'm not a trainer, but I've learned from some great ones, done a lot of research, and figured out even more through pure trial, error, and treats. Everything here comes from real life with real dogs, one scattered day at a time.

Whether you're running on hyperfocus, recharging with your pup, or just embracing the beautiful chaos, you're in the right nest.
